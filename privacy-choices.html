Privacy Choices

Manage Your Privacy Choices

This page can be used as Apple’s optional User Privacy Choices URL and as a public explanation of how people can manage their privacy options with PixieGo.

Inside the App

  • Review or update your emergency contact in Settings.
  • Toggle accessibility and local preference settings in Settings.
  • Use device settings to limit ad personalization where supported by the platform.
  • Use the in-app delete account option to request deletion.
  • Control location access from your device’s system settings.

Outside the App

  • Read our Privacy Policy.
  • Request account deletion through Account Deletion.
  • Submit privacy questions through the PixieGo Support page or Settings > Report a Problem in the app.
