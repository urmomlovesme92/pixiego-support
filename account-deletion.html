Account Deletion

Request Deletion of Your PixieGo Account

This page is intended for the public deletion URL required for apps that allow account creation and are published through Google Play, and it is also useful as Apple’s privacy choices URL.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Open the PixieGo app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. Select Delete My Account.
  4. Follow the confirmation prompts.

If you cannot access the app, use the PixieGo Support page and include the email address associated with your account so we can verify the request.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your PixieGo account will be scheduled for deletion from our active systems.
  • Associated profile information and user-generated content may be deleted or anonymized.
  • Some records may be retained where required by law, security, fraud prevention, or accounting obligations.
  • Deleting your PixieGo account does not automatically cancel app-store subscriptions; those must be cancelled in Apple or Google account settings.

Processing Time

Deletion requests are typically reviewed within 7 business days. If we need additional information to confirm identity or prevent fraud, processing may take longer.

Questions

For questions about deletion, privacy, or data retention, use the PixieGo Support page or Settings > Report a Problem in the app.
