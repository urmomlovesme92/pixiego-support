Common Requests

  • Subscription billing, renewal, and restore help.
  • Login and account access issues.
  • Location permission or routing issues.
  • Bug reports and feature feedback.
  • Crash reports, incorrect restroom pins, and missing restroom reports.
  • Privacy and account deletion requests.

Subscription Help

Subscriptions purchased through the Apple App Store or Google Play are managed by the relevant store. Refunds, billing management, and cancellation rights may depend on that store’s rules and your local law.

  • Apple users should manage subscriptions through Apple ID subscription settings.
  • Google Play users should manage subscriptions through Google Play subscription settings.
  • If a purchase is active but features remain locked, use Settings > Report a Problem and include your platform and account email.

Account Deletion

If you want to request deletion of your PixieGo account and associated data, use the in-app deletion flow or visit our public Account Deletion page.

Before You Contact Us

To help us resolve issues faster, include:

  • Your PixieGo account email address.
  • Your device type and operating system version.
  • Your app version.
  • A short description of the issue and how to reproduce it.
