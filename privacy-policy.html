PixieGo Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how PixieGo collects, uses, stores, shares, retains, and deletes information when people use the PixieGo mobile application and related services.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information such as email address and authentication credentials.
  • Emergency contact information if you choose to store it in the app.
  • Restroom reports, preferences, support requests, or other content you submit.
  • Subscription or purchase-related selections made in the app.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Approximate or precise location data when you grant location permission, including location signals used for route guidance, arrival detection, and park-area pressure guidance.
  • Optional Apple Health activity data processed on your device when you grant Health permission, including step count, heart-rate samples, walking heart-rate average, and active energy used for water, shade, and rest break recommendations. PixieGo does not transmit or retain this HealthKit data on its servers.
  • Device, app, and diagnostic information needed to operate, secure, and improve the service.
  • Subscription status and purchase metadata returned by Apple, Google, and RevenueCat.
  • Locally stored preferences such as accessibility mode, family mode, or saved park settings.

Information We Do Not Intend to Collect

PixieGo is not designed to collect microphone recordings, contact lists, text messages, or photo libraries through the current app build.

How We Use Information

  • Provide the app’s core navigation, park-planning, restroom lookup, and safety features.
  • Estimate route pressure, restroom wait guidance, and crowd color bands without presenting exact guaranteed people counts.
  • Recommend water, shade, and rest breaks using optional Apple Health activity data processed on the device.
  • Authenticate users and maintain account access.
  • Process and restore subscriptions and paid entitlements.
  • Save preferences and accessibility settings across sessions.
  • Respond to support requests and account-related requests, including deletion.
  • Monitor service reliability, prevent abuse, and improve app quality.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

How We Share Information

We may share information with service providers that help us operate PixieGo, including:

  • Supabase for authentication, database, and backend infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat for subscription management and entitlement status.
  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads for free-tier in-app advertising.
  • Apple and Google for in-app purchase processing and restoration.
  • Platform and map providers such as Apple Maps or Google Maps where required by the mobile operating system or map SDK.

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect users or the public, or as part of a merger, financing, acquisition, or sale of assets.

Free users may be shown in-app advertising. Advertising and measurement systems may use device or app identifiers, diagnostic data, and contextual signals as permitted by platform rules and your consent or privacy settings where required.

PixieGo currently intends to request non-personalized ads for free-tier advertising. We do not intend to send precise location to ad networks for targeted advertising.

Optional Apple Health activity data stays on the device and is used only to calculate hydration, shade, and rest suggestions. PixieGo does not sell HealthKit data, use it for advertising, transmit it to its servers, or write data back to Apple Health.

Crowd colors and wait times are guidance estimates. They may be based on available app signals, verified reports, licensed/provider data if configured, and planning estimates. They may be imperfect because one phone can represent a whole family or group. When fresh live signals are not available, PixieGo should show an awaiting-signal state instead of fake live numbers.

Planner actions may open official third-party park websites for reservations, passes, dining, or other park services. Those websites are operated by third parties and are governed by their own privacy policies.

Data Retention and Deletion

We retain personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary to operate the service, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, comply with law, or honor user requests.

  • Locally stored settings may remain on the device until the app is uninstalled or app data is cleared.
  • Account records and backend content may remain until deleted or until retention periods expire.
  • Payment card details are handled by Apple, Google, and their payment systems, not stored directly by PixieGo.

Users can request deletion through the in-app account deletion path and through our public deletion request page at Account Deletion.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect personal information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

  • You can deny or revoke location permissions in your device settings.
  • You can update or remove certain locally stored preferences inside the app.
  • You can cancel subscriptions through Apple App Store or Google Play account settings.
  • You can request access, correction, or deletion by contacting us or using our deletion page.

Children

PixieGo is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to consent to data processing on their own. If you know that a child provided personal information inappropriately, contact us so we can review and address it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the effective date at the top of this page and, where required, provide additional notice.

Contact Us

Privacy questions can be submitted through the PixieGo Support page or through Settings > Report a Problem in the app.
